System Name:
SmartShip Tracker
System Description:
"SmartShip Tracker" is an integrated system that uses GPS and Internet of Things (IoT) technologies to provide
real-time tracking of shipments throughout their journey, from the point of origin to the destination.
How It Works
  1. Tracking Devices
  2. IoT-enabled sensors are attached to containers or shipments to collect critical data, such as:
    • Geolocation (GPS).
    • Temperature.
    • Humidity.
    • Vibrations.
  3. Connectivity:
  4. The devices transmit data via communication networks (Wi-Fi, 4G, or satellite) to a central database.
  5. User Interface
  6. The system provides an easy-to-use interface (mobile app or web platform) for customers and the company, allowing them to:
    • View the shipment’s current location.
    • Receive alerts for any anomalies (e.g., temperature fluctuations or delays).
    • Access detailed shipment status reports.
  7. Smart Analytics
Benefits
  1. Enhanced Transparency
  2. Customers can monitor their shipments in real time, increasing trust and reducing inquiries.
  3. Reduced Losses:
    • Alerts the company to abnormal conditions, such as temperature increases (critical for refrigerated goods).
    • Helps prevent damage or loss through early intervention.
  4. Operational Efficiency:
    • Provides accurate data for optimizing transportation routes.
    • Avoids costly delays by predicting potential issues ahead of time.
